# Fundamental Needs

Fundamental needs are essential requirements for human survival, security, and self-fulfillment. Meeting these needs forms the foundation for well-being, supporting physical health, emotional resilience, and personal growth. When these needs are satisfied, individuals are better equipped to pursue higher-level goals and build meaningful connections.
